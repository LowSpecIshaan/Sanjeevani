Schemes for Migrant Workers in Kerala

Here are some government schemes designed to support migrant workers in Kerala.

1. Aawaz Insurance Scheme

  • Free health insurance up to ₹15,000 per year.
  • Accidental death coverage of ₹2 lakh.
  • Free enrollment for migrant workers aged 18–60 years.
Check Eligibility

2. Apna Ghar Housing Scheme

  • Affordable rental housing at ₹800 per month.
  • Basic facilities like water, electricity, sanitation included.
  • Safe accommodation for migrant workers near industrial hubs.
Check Eligibility

3. Migrant Worker Education Program

  • Free evening classes for children of migrant workers.
  • Bridge courses to help integrate students into Kerala schools.
  • Support in Malayalam, Hindi, and English languages.
Check Eligibility

4. Health Check-up & Mobile Clinics

  • Regular health camps in labor camps and workplaces.
  • Free medical check-ups and medicines.
  • Mobile clinics reaching remote work locations.
Check Eligibility

5. Welfare Board Benefits

  • Financial aid in case of accidents or critical illness.
  • Education scholarships for migrant workers’ children.
  • Pension benefits after long-term registered work.
